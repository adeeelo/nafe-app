Turning mineral or royalty interests into immediate cash. I work for you, the seller, plain and simple. Other mineral brokers are acting on behalf of the end buyer, and are incentivized to purchase your minerals at the lowest price possible. Homestead Mineral Brokers takes your minerals to market, knowing exactly to whom to showcase your assets. As a seller-side broker, my approach is focused squarely on protecting sellers from unfair lowball offers; being a committed partner who walks through the process with sellers end-to-end; and finding the most competitive prices for your mineral interests. I achieve this is by: maintaining an extensive list of pre-qualified buyers who are highly motivated to pay premium prices for interests in your area.

I utilize their expertise to complete a mineral appraisal that will tell you exactly what your mineral rights are worth. If you need a step-up in basis for tax purposes, I can handle that as well.
