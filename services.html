What we Do

Sell Oil & Gas royalties and minerals for a variety of reasons

Consultation

First, a consultation to understand your goals for the mineral sale process.

Reviewing Your Property and Purchase Offers

An initial review of your property. Specifically checking for production, we can get an idea of what your minerals are worth.

Marketing Strategy

We develop a marketing strategy of how to get you the most money for your minerals.

Extensive List Of Buyers

Based on the goals we discuss with you, we field the offers as they come in. Always taking our lead from you but giving advice on potential counter offers.

We Can Partner

If necessary we can partner with our sister company Herco Oil and Gas (www.hercooilandgas.com) to provide you with a mineral appraisal. In cases of inheriting oil and gas royalties or minerals, we can go back to the date you acquired the interest. For tax reasons you’ll want to establish the accounting basis or step-up in basis for the mineral

Contractual Process

We’re there until the very end, ensuring the closing process goes smoothly and that the wired funds are safely in your account before any deed is transferred over to the buyer.
